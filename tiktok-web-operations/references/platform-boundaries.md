# TikTok Platform Boundaries

Use current official TikTok pages as the authority and recheck them when rules may have changed.

## Required baseline

- Community Guidelines: <https://www.tiktok.com/community-guidelines>
- Integrity and authenticity: <https://www.tiktok.com/community-guidelines/en/integrity-authenticity/>
- Accounts and features: <https://www.tiktok.com/community-guidelines/en/accounts-features>
- Creating a post: <https://support.tiktok.com/en/using-tiktok/creating-videos/making-a-post>
- TikTok Studio: <https://support.tiktok.com/en/using-tiktok/creating-videos/tiktok-studio>
- Creative Center: <https://ads.tiktok.com/business/creativecenter>

## Hard boundaries

- Do not use automation to register or operate accounts in bulk, distribute high-volume commercial content, artificially increase engagement signals, or circumvent enforcement.
- Do not buy, sell, trade, coordinate, or promise follows, likes, views, comments, gifts, or other engagement.
- Do not publish copied or minimally changed content without the required rights. Treat visible third-party watermarks as a strong stop signal.
- Do not misrepresent identity, product use, customer results, partnerships, creator relationships, metrics, or location.
- Do not publish repetitive unsolicited promotion in other creators' comments.
- Do not operate another account to bypass a warning, restriction, or ban.

## Disclosure gates

- When promoting Loci, a brand, product, or business, verify TikTok's content disclosure setting for that post.
- Label realistic AI-generated or significantly edited people/scenes when required. A caption, watermark, sticker, or TikTok AIGC label may satisfy disclosure, but verify the current rule and UI.
- Do not use an adult private person's likeness without permission; do not create realistic AIGC likenesses of minors.
- Record music and footage rights. For a business account or commercial post, prefer sounds cleared for commercial use.

## Enforcement signals

Treat these as hard stops for mutation: CAPTCHA, SMS/email/device verification, rate limit, copyright check failure, content/account warning, feature restriction, login mismatch, or a post/comment that may already have submitted but cannot be verified.

Warnings and removals override planned cadence. Do not recover by opening or switching to another account.
